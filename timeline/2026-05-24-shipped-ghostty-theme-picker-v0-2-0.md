I shipped [v0.2.0](https://github.com/NicholasClooney/ghostty-theme-picker/releases/tag/v0.2.0) of [ghostty-theme-picker](https://github.com/NicholasClooney/ghostty-theme-picker), a two-column TUI theme browser for Ghostty that lets me compare dark and light themes side by side, star favorites, and keep jump history while I browse. The new release adds forward history and persistent browse state, which makes the picker feel much more like a tiny terminal-native app instead of a one-shot config helper.
