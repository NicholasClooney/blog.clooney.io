Shipped theme mode control and delayed previews in [v1.25.0](https://github.com/TheClooneyCollection/11ty-subspace-builder/releases/tag/v1.25.0) of 11ty-subspace-builder. The new theme switch adds explicit `auto`, `light`, and `dark` modes instead of treating the site theme as a hidden implementation detail, and delayed previews make hover previews configurable so they feel less jumpy when moving around a page.
